How it works

One clean flow, start to follow-up

SetLinkAI is built around a single, fast loop — so trainers spend time coaching, not wrestling with software.

Step 01

Add a client & build the plan

Create a client profile and put together a structured weekly workout plan with sets, reps, rest, and coaching notes.

Step 02

Client opens a private link

Each client gets their own mobile-friendly link. They see today's workout, check off exercises, log reps and weight, and rate difficulty.

Step 03

You see status & the next move

Check-ins roll up into a color-coded accountability view with a suggested follow-up message ready to send.

The difference

Accountability Autopilot

Every client is sorted into a simple status so you always know who's thriving and who needs a check-in — with a follow-up message drafted for you.

🟢

Green — On track

Completed the expected workouts and check-ins with no concerns. Keep the momentum going.

🟡

Yellow — Missed one

Skipped a single session or check-in. A quick, friendly nudge usually gets them right back.

🟠

Orange — Struggling

Multiple misses, very hard workouts, or low effort. Time to adjust the plan around real life.

🔴

Red — Inactive

No activity for 7+ days or repeated misses. Reach out and reset the plan so it feels doable.

⚑

Pain & discomfort flags

When a client marks discomfort during a workout, it's surfaced clearly so you can swap a movement out.

✶

Difficulty signals

Too easy or too hard ratings flow straight into your view, so the next session lands at the right intensity.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Do my clients need to download an app?+
No. Each client gets a private, mobile-friendly web link. They open it in their browser, see today's workout, and check off exercises — no account creation or app install needed. They can add it to their home screen if they'd like an app-like feel.
What is Accountability Autopilot?+
It's the part of SetLinkAI that sorts each client into a simple color status — green, yellow, orange, or red — based on completed workouts, check-ins, difficulty ratings, and pain flags. For each status it suggests a follow-up message you can send, so staying on top of your roster takes minutes, not hours.
How does the Free Trial work?+
The Free Trial is request-based. Submit the early access form and SetLinkAI will follow up with next steps for testing the one-client trial flow. Paid plans can be subscribed to directly through secure Stripe checkout.
What's the difference between trainer plans and direct client plans?+
Trainer plans are your subscription to SetLinkAI, priced by how many clients you manage. Direct client plans are subscriptions a client pays to follow a plan you've assigned — either a self-guided Interactive plan or a Daily Accountability plan with ongoing follow-up.
Can I change or cancel my plan later?+
Yes. Plan changes and cancellations can be handled through the billing flow once your subscription is active. See the Cancellation Policy and Refund Policy linked in the footer for the full details on changes, billing, and cancellations.
Does SetLinkAI give medical or nutrition advice?+
No. SetLinkAI is fitness planning and coaching software for trainers and their clients. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, supplements, medication, or healthcare services. See the disclaimer below for more.
