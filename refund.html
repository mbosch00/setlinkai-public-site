Refund Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Subscription refund rule: Refund requests must be submitted within seven (7) business days of the charge. After seven (7) business days from the charge date, SetLinkAI subscriptions are non-refundable except where required by law or approved for limited billing issues.

1. Digital Subscription Service

SetLinkAI provides digital subscription access to software and related onboarding/support workflows. Because access is delivered digitally and subscription access can begin shortly after payment, refunds are limited as described in this policy.

2. Refund Window

Unless a separate written agreement states otherwise, refund requests must be submitted within seven (7) business days of the applicable subscription charge. After seven (7) business days from the charge date, the payment is non-refundable. Refund requests submitted within the refund window are reviewed case by case and are not guaranteed unless required by law.

3. Non-Refundable Items

  • Monthly subscription payments after the seven (7) business day window.
  • Renewal charges that were not canceled before the billing date.
  • Periods where access was available but not used.
  • Partial months, unused time, or downgrades made after a billing cycle begins.
  • Payments made by trainers, companies, studios, or clients who later decide not to use the service.

4. Exceptions We May Review

We may review refund requests for duplicate charges, clear billing errors, accidental payments reported promptly, unauthorized transactions reported through the appropriate payment process, or circumstances where a refund is required by law.

5. Failed Payments and Access

If a subscription payment fails, access to SetLinkAI, trainer portals, client links, or related features may be paused, restricted, or disabled until the subscription is brought current.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email max.bosch00@gmail.com with your name, billing email, plan name, charge date, and reason for the request.

7. Processing Time

If approved, refunds are generally submitted back to the original payment method. Bank and card processing times may vary and are controlled by the payment processor or financial institution.

8. Relationship to Cancellation Policy

Canceling a subscription stops future renewals; it does not automatically create a refund for a past or current billing period. See the Cancellation Policy for more information.
