Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Cancel before renewal: Customers are responsible for canceling before the next billing date to avoid future charges. Cancellation stops future renewals but does not automatically refund current or past charges.

1. Monthly Subscriptions

SetLinkAI subscriptions are billed on a recurring monthly basis unless otherwise stated at checkout or in a written agreement. By subscribing, you authorize recurring charges until the subscription is canceled.

2. How to Cancel

Customers may request cancellation by contacting max.bosch00@gmail.com or by using any customer billing portal or cancellation method made available through the payment processor. Include your name, billing email, and subscription plan so we can locate the account.

3. Timing of Cancellation

Cancellations should be submitted before the next billing date. If a cancellation request is received after a renewal has processed, the charge may be subject to the Refund Policy and may be non-refundable after seven (7) business days.

4. Access After Cancellation

Unless access is terminated earlier due to misuse, failed payment, violation of Terms, or security reasons, customers may retain access through the end of the paid billing period. After the billing period ends, access to trainer portals, client links, dashboards, workout plans, or other subscription features may be disabled.

5. Trainer-Managed Clients

If a trainer subscription is canceled, past due, or disabled, client links and client access connected to that trainer account may also be paused, locked, or disabled. Trainer-created client access is dependent on the trainer’s active subscription unless a separate direct client subscription has been created.

6. Direct Client Subscriptions

If a direct client subscription is canceled or payment fails, only that direct client’s subscription access may be paused, locked, or disabled, unless another agreement applies.

7. Downgrades and Plan Changes

Plan changes, upgrades, or downgrades may take effect immediately or at the next billing period depending on the billing setup. Downgrades may require reducing client count or feature access to match the new plan.

8. No Automatic Refund

Cancellation does not automatically refund current or past charges. Refunds, if any, are governed by the Refund Policy.

9. Contact

Cancellation questions can be sent to max.bosch00@gmail.com.
