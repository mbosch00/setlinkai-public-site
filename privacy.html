Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Privacy commitment: SetLinkAI is designed to protect trainer, client, and contact information using reasonable safeguards. We avoid collecting unnecessary information and use information to operate and support the service.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how SetLinkAI and Bosch Productions collect, use, store, share, and protect information when you visit our website, submit an access request, pay for a subscription, or use SetLinkAI-related services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, company or business name, and support messages.
  • Trainer information: trainer name, brand, plan interest, client count, business information, coaching notes, and workspace details.
  • Client information: client names, goals, workout schedules, plan details, check-ins, progress notes, difficulty ratings, discomfort flags, and information entered by a trainer, client, or authorized user.
  • Payment and subscription information: plan selected, subscription status, billing details, customer identifiers, invoices, and payment confirmations. Full card details are processed by third-party payment providers such as Stripe and are not stored by SetLinkAI.
  • Technical information: device, browser, IP address, pages visited, timestamps, and security or log data needed to operate and protect the service.

3. How We Use Information

  • Respond to early access requests, onboarding requests, support messages, and customer questions.
  • Create, manage, and support trainer, client, and subscription workflows.
  • Operate, maintain, improve, troubleshoot, secure, and personalize SetLinkAI.
  • Process payments, manage subscription status, and communicate billing information.
  • Detect abuse, prevent unauthorized access, protect user data, and comply with legal obligations.

4. How We Protect Information

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards intended to protect contact information, trainer information, client information, subscription information, and account information. These safeguards may include restricted access, secure third-party infrastructure, encrypted connections where available, limited data use, access controls, and monitoring for misuse.

No internet-based service, website, email system, or database can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for keeping access links, account credentials, and client information confidential.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell trainer or client information. We may share information only as needed to operate the service, including with service providers for hosting, email delivery, payment processing, analytics, security, support, and infrastructure; payment processors such as Stripe; authorized users intended to access a workspace, portal, or client plan; and where required for legal or safety reasons.

6. Trainer and Client Data Responsibilities

Trainers and organizations are responsible for having appropriate permission to enter or upload client information into SetLinkAI. Trainers should avoid entering sensitive medical information unless necessary for the coaching workflow and authorized by the client. SetLinkAI is not intended to serve as an electronic medical record, healthcare record system, or regulated healthcare provider system.

7. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, manage subscriptions, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain security, and support business records. We may delete or anonymize information when it is no longer needed.

8. Your Choices

You may request access, correction, deletion, or review of certain personal information by contacting us. Some information may be retained where necessary for legal, billing, security, backup, fraud prevention, or legitimate business purposes.

9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

SetLinkAI may use cookies or similar technologies to operate the website, remember preferences, understand usage, protect against abuse, and improve the service.

10. Children and Minors

SetLinkAI is not intended for children under 13. Users under 18 should use SetLinkAI only with involvement and permission from a parent, guardian, trainer, coach, or responsible adult. Trainers and organizations are responsible for ensuring proper consent before entering information about minors.

11. Third-Party Links

SetLinkAI may link to third-party services such as Stripe, Netlify, Resend, or other providers. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this Privacy Policy.

12. Changes and Contact

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Privacy questions or requests can be sent to max.bosch00@gmail.com.
